Lists

Our software allows you to build your own lists and add each dataset to one or multiple lists. Lists can be a powerful tool when combining data sets. Imagine one of your companies is in the pet niche and you need leads for your pet brand.

You extract the user followers from the top 30 pet accounts on Twitter and add them to your 'pet list'. Our software will then identify which users are found the most times in each of the 30 pet Twitter accounts. The list table for your pet company in our software is now sorted by the users most interested in pets, ensuring you can reach out to those users first and generate sales fast.

How it Works?

Step 1

Search Twitter for your target audience and Twitter accounts that have authority in your niche and copy their Twitter usernames.

Step 2

Enter the Twitter usernames in our software and select if you want to extract their user followers, user following or both. Decide how many users to extract, and click start.

Step 3

Our software will run and extract every user, match these users to our vast database and allow you to download all results to CSV. You will be notified when your data is complete and ready to download.
