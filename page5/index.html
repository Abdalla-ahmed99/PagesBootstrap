Lists

Our software allows you to build your own lists and add each dataset to one or multiple lists. Lists can be a powerful tool when combining data sets. Imagine one of your companies is in the pet niche and you need leads for your pet brand. You extract the user followers from the top 30 pet accounts on Twitter and add them to your 'pet list'. Our software will then identify which users are found the most times in each of the 30 pet Twitter accounts. The list table for your pet company in our software is now sorted by the users most interested in pets, ensuring you can reach out to those users first and generate sales fast.
